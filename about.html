About us

Our philosophy

Florience was born from a love of storytelling—through flowers. What began as a small creative pursuit soon flourished into a full-fledged floral design studio, bringing artistry and emotion into every arrangement. Inspired by nature's effortless beauty and the way florals can define a moment, we set out to craft designs that feel organic yet intentional, timeless yet personal. Every bouquet, every installation, and every petal is chosen with purpose, ensuring each piece tells its own story. Today, Florience is more than a floral studio—it's a place where dreams take shape in the form of delicate blooms and cascading arrangements. Rooted in creativity and driven by passion, we continue to push the boundaries of floral artistry, designing for those who seek something truly unforgettable.
