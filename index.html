About our studio

Founded in 2009, Florience is a boutique floral design studio in Surrey, London, creating exquisite, handcrafted arrangements for weddings and events. Led by Isabelle Laurent, our studio blends creativity with nature's beauty to craft elegant and timeless designs.

What began as a passion project has flourished into a sought-after destination for floral artistry, bringing breathtaking visions to life for clients across the UK and beyond
